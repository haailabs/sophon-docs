The Sophon Farm is designed to allow users to contribute to Sophon's growth while earning rewards. 

To reward the loyal community, 10% of the $SOPH token supply will be dedicated to early supporters through the Sophon Farm. 

By depositing assets into pools (like ETH, BEAM and more), you'll accumulate Sophon Points (SP) with every new block. Sophon Farm will initially be available on Ethereum Mainnet, followed by a second farming period on Sophon Mainnet.
